Introduction
Better365 (“the App,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Better365 mobile application.

By creating an account or using Better365, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect a. Information You Provide
We may collect the following information when you use the App:

Account Information: Email address and password.

Profile Details: Optional personal preferences you choose to provide.

Goal & Activity Data: Goals, tasks, progress updates, streaks, learning topics, and reflection/mirror notes.

We do not collect sensitive personal information unless you voluntarily submit it (e.g., notes written in your accountability mirror).

b. Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect:

Device information (model, OS version, app version).

Usage data (features accessed, session length).

Diagnostic data for improving stability and performance.

Better365 does not collect precise location data.

c. Tracking Technologies

If your app uses NSUserTrackingUsageDescription (required by Apple), we must state:

Better365 does not track users across third-party apps or websites

Better365 does not use advertising identifiers (IDFA)

Personalize your in-app experience

Provide user support

Maintain the security and integrity of the app
